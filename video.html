BWI Revamped

My project with BWI, October 01, 2020

My Macgyverism..

I saw this project and knew it was for me to tear apart. I took my Arduino Uno kit with a breadboard and mixed it with the Puzzlebox Orbit electronics. I preprogrammed the mcu to say brain power:). When the EEG headset started communicating with the Arduino Uno the screen would show what I had programmed it to state.
